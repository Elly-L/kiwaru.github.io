Streamlining Potato Sales through Digital platform

Revolutionizing the potato value chain in Kenya by empowering farmers, improving market efficiency, and enhancing the overall market experience.

Project Description

Problem Statement

Small-scale potato farmers in Kenya often face challenges in selling their produce efficiently and profitably. The challenges include:

  • Limited Market Reach: This restrictsfarmers' ability to reach a wider customer base, potentially leading to lower prices and inconsistent income.
  • Lack of Information Transparency: Farmers may not have access to real-time market data on potato prices, leading to situations where they are unaware of the true value of their harvest and potentially accept lower prices.
  • Limited Customer Connection: Direct communication with consumers is often challenging, making it difficult for farmers to understand customer preferences and adapt their offerings accordingly.

KI-WARU Mobile App

The platform's essential features are crafted to simplify the trading process, provide valuable opportunities for all vendors and Retailer to increase their income, and provide consumer ease access to fresh produce.

Key Features

  • Flexible Signup/Login: The registration process is simplified by enabling users to register as vendors or buyers at one go.
  • Shopping panel: The shop page allows filtering of products based on Geo-location and Price range.
  • Product customization: Vendors can efficiently curate their product posts through the picture upload feature and add product information and their contacts.
  • Blog Page: The presence of a Blog site to provide both vendors and buyers with the latest information about market trends,new farming technologies, and other relevant agro-facts.
  • Notification alerts: Vendors get push alerts when buyers place orders and Buyers get notified when Vendors accept/drop their orders.
